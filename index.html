What is Toastmasters?

Toastmasters International is a world-leading communication and leadership organisation with over 300,000 members in 145 countries. Members grow their confidence, sharpen their public speaking skills, and discover their voice — in a supportive, non-judgmental community.

🎤

Public speaking

Prepare and deliver speeches in a safe, encouraging environment at every meeting.

💬

Impromptu speaking

Build confidence thinking on your feet with Table Topics — short, spontaneous speeches.

🏆

Leadership

Take on club roles and grow your leadership skills alongside your speaking ability.

🌍

Community

Connect with a diverse, welcoming group of people who share your goals.

Division C

Our clubs

Division C comprises the Germany Middle East region, bringing together Toastmasters clubs from Berlin, Hannover, Leipzig, Dresden and beyond. Each club meets regularly — visit as a guest before committing. All are welcome.
