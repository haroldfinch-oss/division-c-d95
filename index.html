What's happening in Division C

Wanderpokal | Traveling Cup
🏆 Currently at: Center Berlin Toastmasters

To earn the trophy, another club has to visit the current trophy holder. During the visit, one member delivers a prepared speech while another serves as the General Evaluator. This initiative was designed to promote collaboration, mutual learning, and valuable feedback between clubs. The trophy was originally introduced by Jürgen Hall during his term as Area Director. Its purpose was to encourage exchange and strengthen the sense of community between clubs.

The rules

  • Two members of a club must attend a club night of the club that currently has the traveling trophy.
  • One member must give a speech.
  • The other member must do the overall judging.
  • Then they can take the cup back to their club and proudly present it. Until it moves on again :)
